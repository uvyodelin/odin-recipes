Pasta

Chicken Aglio Pasta is a delicious and satisfying Italian dish that combines tender chicken pieces with al dente pasta and a flavorful garlic-infused olive oil sauce. The name "aglio" comes from the Italian word for garlic, which is a key ingredient in this dish, along with olive oil and sometimes chili flakes for a hint of spice.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (225 grams) of spaghetti or linguine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or chicken thighs), sliced into thin strips
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley or basil, chopped (for garnish)
